Very Rare Gauntlets

*Covered in gilded scales, amplifying natural strength.*

Grants +1 to STR-based skill checks. 
Apply Dawnmark when you Grapple or punch an enemy (1/turn).

**Dawnmark:** Stackable condition. On attack, you may consume all stacks to deal additional Radiant damage based on the amount of marks consumed this way (1d4>1d6> ... >1d20).
