Very Rare Amulet

*Made to banish encroaching dark.*

Grants the ability to cast Warding Bond for free (1/encounter).
When you or your ward is attacked, apply Dawnmark to the attacker (1/turn).

**Dawnmark:** Stackable condition. On attack, you may consume all stacks to deal additional Radiant damage according to the amount of marks consumed this way (1d4>1d6> ... >1d20).
