Legendary Plate Armor

*The intoxicating power of dread still churns within the blackened armor of a long-deposed king.*

20 Armor (Req. 4 STR).
When rolling Initiative, all enemies within Reach 4 must make a WIL save (DC 10+KEY). On fail, they become Frightened for 1 minute or until damaged. If they fail by 5 or more, they also become Incapacitated for 1 turn.

*(by Can Opener)*
