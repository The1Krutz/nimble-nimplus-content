Very Rare Crossbow

*Ornate crossbow with engravings of resplendent saints.*

4d4+DEX Piercing. 2-handed, Load: 1 action, Range 8.
On hit: Apply Dawnmark (1/turn).
When used to consume Dawnmark, the additional Radiant damage also hits enemies in Line 3 behind the target.

**Dawnmark:** Stackable condition. On attack, you may consume all stacks to deal additional Radiant damage according to the amount of marks consumed this way (1d4>1d6> ... >1d20).
