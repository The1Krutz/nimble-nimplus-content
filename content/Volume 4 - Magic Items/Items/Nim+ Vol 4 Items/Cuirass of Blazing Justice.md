Very Rare Plate Armor

*Gold-trimmed breastplate emblazoned with a motif of the rising sun.*

18 Armor (Req. 3 STR).
When you Defend or Interpose, apply Dawnmark to the attacker (1/turn).
All Dawnmarked undead & unholy creatures within Reach 3 have disadvantage on all rolls. 

**Dawnmark:** Stackable condition. On attack, you may consume all stacks to deal additional Radiant damage based on the amount of marks consumed this way (1d4>1d6> ... >1d20).
