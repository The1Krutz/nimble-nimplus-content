*The dwarves of Kral Vetu once were legendary for their mastery of runic inscriptions, able to grant power to even the most ordinary of equipment. Though the craft was thought to be lost with the disappearance of the Dverung themselves, intrepid explorers have been known to uncover some of the few that remain.*

# How to use the Dverung Runes
Dverung Runes are small runes inscribed on a rare mineral from Kral Vetu. These runes can be melded into most types of equipment, granting the equipment special properties and consuming the rune in the process. The more powerful the equipment, the more runes it's able to contain.
- Common & Uncommon - 1 Rune
- Rare & Very Rare - 2 Runes
- Legendary - 3 Runes

# Weapon Runes
1. **Elemental Rune.** Adds 1d4 Fire, Ice or Lightning damage.
2. **Divinity Rune.** Adds 1d4 Radiant or Necrotic damage.
3. **Animosity Rune.** Adds 1d6 damage. On miss: you take this damage instead.
4. **Slaying Rune.** Adds 1d4 damage against a specific type of creature.
5. **Wounding Rune.** You may suffer 1d6 damage to add twice that amount on attack.
6. **Gnatbane Rune.** Increase Primary Die by 1 when attacking Small or Tiny creatures.
7. **Returning Rune.** Grants the ability to return to your hand after being thrown.
8. **Light Rune.** Able to glow brightly on command.
9. **Hammer Rune.** Pushes target 1 space on hit.
10. **Death Rune.** Grants 5 temp HP on kill.

# Armor Runes
1. **Protection Rune.** Adds +1 armor.
2. **Fortitude Rune.** Grants resistance to one type of damage.
3. **Endurance Rune.** Adds 5 max HP and +1 inventory slot.
4. **Illusion Rune.** Impose disadvantage on an attack against you (1/encounter).
5. **Thorn Rune.** Deal 1 damage to melee attackers.
6. **Speed Rune.** Grants +1 Speed.
7. **Fish Rune.** Grants waterbreathing and a swim Speed.
8. **Feather Rune.** Slows falling and grants immunity to fall damage.
9. **Stealth Rune.** Grants advantage on Stealth rolls.
10. **Healing Rune.** Increases healing by 1d6.
