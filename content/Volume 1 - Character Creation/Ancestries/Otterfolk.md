_Playful, friendly, and always up for some well-intended mischief, Otterfolk are at home both in water and on land. Although they can be fierce warriors, they typically prefer using their environment to their advantage._

**At Home in Water**
You have a swim Speed as long as you are wearing armor no heavier than leather. When Field Resting in water, you regain additional HP equal to your level.
