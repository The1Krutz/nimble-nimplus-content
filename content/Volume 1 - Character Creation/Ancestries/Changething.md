(Small or Medium)

*Unassuming humanoids with the uncanny ability to disguise themselves as inanimate objects, perhaps due to a curse or a mimic ancestor.*

**Mimicry**
(KEY/Safe Rest) Action: Disguise yourself as a harmless inanimate object of the same size as you or smaller. You move at half Speed while disguised. Ends if you take damage, attack or cast a spell.
