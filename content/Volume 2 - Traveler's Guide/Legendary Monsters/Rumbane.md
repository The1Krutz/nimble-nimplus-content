*Level 3 Drunken Fist & Also A Dragon*

**HP:** 25/hero | **Armor:** Medium | **Saves:** DEX+

> **Drunken Master.** Permanently Tipsy. Heavy Armor while Prone.

---

After each hero's turn, move 6 and choose one:
* **Dragon Fist.** 1d4+12 Slashing. On crit: Enemies adjacent to the target take 5 Fire damage.
* **Rancid Breath.** Daze an adjacent creature. DC 11 STR save or become Tipsy.
* **Mighty Belch.** (1 use, Cone 4) 3d10+5 Fire, half on a DC 11 DEX save.

> **Tipsy.** Attacks miss on a roll of 1 or 2. Fall Prone when missing an attack.

---

**BLOODIED:** Rumbane transforms into a Large dragon and gains 2 uses of Mighty Belch.
**LAST STAND (30):** Rumbane is exhausted and really wants a nap! He gains Heavy Armor and Dragon Fist rolls with advantage. Falls asleep after 1 round if not defeated before then.
