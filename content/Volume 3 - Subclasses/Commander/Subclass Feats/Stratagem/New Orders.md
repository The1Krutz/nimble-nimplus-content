Add Watch Out!, Hit The Weak Spot! and Shake It Off! to the list of available	Commander's Orders. Choose another Commander's Order.

---

**Watch Out!** 1 action. (1/encounter) Reaction (when an ally within Reach 12 is attacked): Cause the attack targeting your ally to miss.

**Hit The Weak Spot!** 1 action. Reaction (as an ally within Reach 12 attacks an enemy): 		Your ally's attack ignores Armor.

**Shake It Off!** 1 action. Action/Reaction (on an ally's turn): Target ally within Reach 12 is cured of either Charmed, Dazed, Frightened or Taunted.
